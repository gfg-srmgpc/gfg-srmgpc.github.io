Welcome to GeeksforGeeks SRMGPC

GeeksforGeeks student Chapters are university-based community chapters for students interested in Computer Science and who want to make their careers in this field. By joining a GeeksforGeeks student Chapter, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build effective and optimized solutions for local businesses and their community.
